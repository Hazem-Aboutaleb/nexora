# PRODUCT

## Product Name

Nexora

---

# Product Category

AI-Powered Surveillance Intelligence Platform

---

# Product Description

Nexora transforms traditional surveillance systems into intelligent systems capable of searching, understanding and analyzing recorded video.

It works with existing CCTV infrastructure without replacing hardware.

---

# Primary Users

- Business Owners
- Security Managers
- Store Managers
- Operations Managers

---

# Main Problem

Businesses own cameras but cannot efficiently use the recorded data.

Finding a specific event is slow and expensive.

---

# Core Value

Turn recorded video into searchable business intelligence.

---

# MVP Features

## Camera Connection

Connect to existing DVR/NVR devices.

---

## Camera Discovery

Display all available cameras.

---

## Recorded Video Access

Read recorded footage from cameras.

---

## AI Search

Search recordings using natural language.

Examples:

- Find the man wearing a blue shirt.
- Show everyone after 8 PM.
- Find a person carrying a backpack.

---

## Smart Timeline

Jump directly to the exact matching timestamp.
