# Nexora Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 0.1.0

Status: Draft

Last Updated: 2026-07-05

Owner: Nexora

---

# 1. Overview

Nexora is an AI platform that transforms existing CCTV systems into intelligent business and security platforms without replacing cameras, DVRs, or NVRs.

The platform connects to existing surveillance systems, analyzes video streams using artificial intelligence, indexes events, and provides search, analytics, alerts, and business insights.

---

# 2. Vision

To become the AI layer for every surveillance system in the world.

Instead of replacing surveillance hardware, Nexora upgrades existing infrastructure using software only.

---

# 3. Mission

Enable businesses to unlock valuable operational and business insights from their existing CCTV systems.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Current CCTV systems only provide:

- Live View
- Recording
- Playback

Finding events requires manually reviewing hours of footage.

Businesses cannot answer questions such as:

- Who entered the store?
- How many customers visited today?
- Which products attracted the most attention?
- How long was the queue?
- How often do employees leave their workstations?
- What happened yesterday at 3 PM?

Video exists.

Information does not.

---

# 5. Solution

Nexora connects to existing surveillance systems and adds an AI layer capable of:

- Video indexing
- Natural language search
- Customer analytics
- Employee analytics
- Security event detection
- Business intelligence dashboards
- Smart reports
- AI assistant

No hardware replacement is required.

---

# 6. Product Principles

Nexora must always follow these principles.

## 1. Software Only

No additional hardware.

## 2. Hardware Independent

Support any DVR, NVR or IP Camera whenever technically possible.

## 3. AI First

Every feature should use AI to create measurable value.

## 4. Five Minute Setup

Installation should be simple enough for any technician.

## 5. Modular

Every feature should be an independent module.

## 6. Privacy First

Customer videos remain under customer control.

## 7. Business Driven

Every feature must solve a real business problem.

# 8. MVP Scope

Version 0.1 includes only:

- Connect to DVR
- Display Cameras
- Video Playback
- Video Indexing
- Natural Language Search

Nothing else.

---

---

# 10. Out Of Scope

The first release will NOT include:

- Face Recognition
- License Plate Recognition
- Attendance System
- POS Integration
- ERP Integration

These features will be developed later.

---

# 11. Success Metrics

The MVP will be considered successful when:

- User installs Nexora in less than five minutes.
- Nexora connects to an existing DVR.
- User searches using natural language.
- Results appear in less than ten seconds.

---

# 12. Long-Term Vision

Nexora will evolve from an AI CCTV platform into a Business Intelligence Platform powered by computer vision.
